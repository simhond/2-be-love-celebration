Welcome to the Tu B'(L)ov Celebration & Sacred Union of Prema and Simhon!

Four years of a shared journey — a love that grew from opposite ends of the country and bridged every distance — have brought us to this moment. We are thrilled to invite you to the ceremony of our sacred union: a white, wild and deeply connected celebration in the heart of nature.

As the sun sets and the moon rises, we will gather under the open sky for an evening where the Four Elements of the universe come together. This will be a night of connection, healing, live music and fire, embraced by the family and friends who are our heart.

Our dress code is simple: come, dance and celebrate in white — and all colours are welcome! The celebration is a potluck — everyone brings something plant-based from the heart, and together we create a feast of joy. After the ceremony we'll continue with a music circle, blessings and dancing into the night, with an energy of freedom and so much love.

And don't miss the Abundance Table waiting for you further down the page 🍽️

We are waiting for you with excitement,
Prema & Simhon 🌕

Staying overnight?

If you're planning to sleep under the stars — wonderful! We'd love to know in advance so we can organize the space accordingly. Please let us know ahead of time if you plan to stay.

What to bring:

  • Tent / lightweight sleeping bag suitable for summer heat
  • Pillow and sleeping mat
  • Personal toiletries — bathrooms and a shower are available on site
  • Everything you need for the night
  • Food for breakfast

The Abundance Table – What will you bring?

Our wedding is rooted in community and sharing, and we invite you to be part of creating a generous, diverse and love-filled table.

Please choose one category and bring a plant-based dish in a quantity that serves approximately 10–15 guests.

If you're coming as a family and would like to contribute more, we'd be delighted if you brought two dishes (from the same or different categories).

To help us maintain variety and balance on the menu, please also specify what exactly you're planning to bring within your chosen category (e.g. tabbouleh, mushroom quiche, focaccia, lentil dal, cheesecake, etc.).

We'd love it if you brought your dish in a beautiful serving vessel 🌿
